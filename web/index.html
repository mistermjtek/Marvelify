[ features ]

Customize

You get to choose what profile you want would like to display and what info you would like to share. Your profile picture is not shared or displayed with anyone immediately so your anonomity is preserved. You get to choose who you want to chat with and if you chat long enough with them, your profile picture will then be revealved.

Connect

The profile picture you choose is initially hidden by a random marvel character. The same goes with other people. We strongly believe that meeting other people should be based on their interests and personality rather than their looks, so your profile picture is initally anonymized. This way you can meet other people without having to worry about your looks and can choose to meet other people based on your shared interests.

Discover

Chat with other people and discover other people's interests and passions. The longer you chat with the other person, the more you find out about them, not just through the information they share, but also through the profile picture they uploaded. As you chat with the other person, their Marvel profile picture slowly chips away to reveal their real image. This creates incentive to chat with the other person to get to know them and also makes the conversation more exciting.
